Confirm your identity

To pay for your apprenticeships using government funds we need to verify your identity.

To do this you will need to add your organisation's PAYE scheme.

You can do this with either:

  • the Government Gateway login for your PAYE scheme
  • your accounts office reference number (AORN) and PAYE scheme - found on the letter HMRC sent you when you first registered as an employer or your accountant will have this information

If you don't do this now you can still do something but you can't access government funding until you do it.
