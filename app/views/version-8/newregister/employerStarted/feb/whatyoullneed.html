Verify your organisation's identity

To pay for your apprenticeships using government funding and to fully access your apprenticeship account you will need to verify your organisation.

To do this you will need:

  • at least one PAYE scheme
  • the Government Gateway login details for your PAYE scheme
Where do I find my Government Gateway login?
Your payroll or finance department should have your organisation’s Government Gateway information if you don’t already know it. If your organisation has a payroll agent, don’t use the agent’s login details. You will need to set up a Government Gateway account if you do not already have one.

If you don’t have this information, you can still register an account and verify your organisation later.
