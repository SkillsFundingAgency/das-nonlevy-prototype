Manage apprenticeships

This service is for organisations that want to take on apprentices.

Use this service to:

  • set up and manage apprenticeship training

Before you start

You will need:

  • the legal name of your organisation for example, the name you've used to register with Companies House
  • your Government Gateway login details or your accounts office reference number (AORN) and at least one PAYE scheme
  • the authority to enter an agreement with the Education and Skills Funding Agency (ESFA)
