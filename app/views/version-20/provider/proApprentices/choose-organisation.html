What is a contract for services?

A contract for services is a formal agreement between a buyer and a supplier.

In this case, the contract is between the organisation that's buying apprenticeship training and assessment for their apprentices and the training provider or end-point assessment organisaion that's training or assessing the apprentices.

If you're an employer-provider, for example, you won't need a contract for services for training as your organisation is both the buyer and the supplier, but you will need one for end-point assessment.
