Employing an apprentice

You must meet the following rules in order to employ an apprentice.

Assurance Aerospace Ltd confirm that:

  • Assurance Aerospace Ltd will meet the cost of the apprenticeâ€™s wages
  • Assurance Aerospace Ltd will provide a job role to the apprentice that allows them to gain the knowledge, skills and behaviours they need to successfully complete the apprenticeship
  • Assurance Aerospace Ltd will give appropriate support and supervision on the job to allow the apprentice to carry out their role
  • The apprentice will have a contract of service with Assurance Aerospace Ltd or an apprenticeship training agency, that is long enough for them to successfully complete the apprenticeship and any end point assessment
    See exceptions for this

    Apprentices who have been made redundant less than six months before the final day of their apprenticeship

    Apprentices who have been made redundant, with more than six months but less than 12 months of their apprenticeship remaining; these apprentices may seek a further contract of service with a duration of less than 12 months, and we will fund them for up to 12 weeks while they do so.

    Particular office holders, namely constables of English police forces and ministers or trainee ministers of a religious denomination, who are undertaking an alternative English apprenticeship. Except for the requirement to have an apprenticeship agreement and a contract of service in relation to these alternative apprentices, you must comply with all the other rules set out in this document.
