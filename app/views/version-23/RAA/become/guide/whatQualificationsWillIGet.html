What qualifications will I get

Levels of apprenticeship

Apprenticeships have equivalent educational levels.

Name Level Equivalent educational level
Intermediate 2 GCSE
Advanced 3 A level
Higher 4,5,6 and 7 Foundation degree and above
Degree 6 and 7 Bachelor’s or master’s degree

Some apprenticeships may also give you an additional qualification, such as a diploma.

Which level should you choose

You will normally do an apprenticeship at the next level up from your current qualifications. You can do the same level as you current qualifications if it is different enough from your "old studies".

We can help you choose which level is right for you.
