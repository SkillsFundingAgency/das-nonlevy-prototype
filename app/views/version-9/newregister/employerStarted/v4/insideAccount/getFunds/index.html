Get funding for taking on an apprentice

Use this service to secure government funding for training an apprentice.

Government funding will pay for part, or all of the cast of training an apprentice. Go to apprenticeship funding to find out how much you are allowed.

Before you start you will need:

  • an estimate of which month the apprentice will start their apprenticeship training, which can be flexible up to 3 months
  • an idea of the apprenticeship training the apprentice will take (optional)

You can only get funding up to 6 months before an apprenticeship is about to start.
