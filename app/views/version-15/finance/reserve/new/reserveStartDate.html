When will the apprentice start their apprenticeship training?

The apprentice can start their apprenticeship training anytime in the month you choose, or the following 2 months. For example if you select January, the apprentice can start their training anytime from January to March.

I want a later start date
You can secure funding no more than 6 months in advance of the apprenticeship training start date. If you want to secure funding for a later date, come back later.
