Register for a manage apprenticeships account

Add your organisation’s PAYE scheme details in order to get government funding for training your apprentices.

You can do this by:

  • granting us authority to use your tax details through HMRC’s Government Gateway

    • Or

  • giving us your accounts office reference number and employer PAYE scheme reference
How to find your Government Gateway login

Ask your payroll or finance department if you do not know your organisation's login details.

If you have a payroll agent, you must set up your own account rather than use their details.

How to find your accounts office reference number
You can find this number on the letter HMRC sent when you first registered as an employer. It is 13 characters. For example, 123PA00045678
How to find your employer reference number

You can find your employer’s reference number on any payslip, P45, P60 or P11D issued to employees, or any letters from HMRC about PAYE.

The number is in two parts:

  • 3 digits to identify the HMRC office number that deals with your PAYE
  • a forward slash followed by the tax office's reference for your organisation

Alternatively, you can register an account which will limit you to just finding and saving apprenticeship training courses and providers.
